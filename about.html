About us

ABOUT SPEIS PRO

SPEIS Architecture is an award-winning LEED Accredited Minority and Small Businesses Enterprise with Architectural Licenses in Nigeria, Abuja. Our location, centrally located in Abuja affords us easy access to Other part of the country.

WHERE WE COME FROM

With a strong background in both Architecture and Construction as well as different types of delivery methods; Design-Bid-Build and Design-Build-Construction, our firm has a solid knowledge base in all facets of project development.

Our firm’s vision is the genesis of two architects who are actively engaged in the development process in order to achieve our clients’ needs. Our insight and knowledge of the financial implications and constraints, associated with any type of development, reinforces our role as your key partner in the entire process.

WHAT WE BELIEVE

SPEIS PRO Architecture stands proudly on our core values and ethics. We believe our clients deserve the highest quality work.

We also believe that we are part of your evolution, an instrumental cog in the process. We’ll be there with you, from the initial schematic design to the grand opening day as your partner and ensure that we provide you with our continuous support.

We focus our design to meet our client’s particular requirements while adjusting for each site’s inherent differences. We approach each project as a unique challenge, and we strive to fit our client’s specific desires and needs to each and every project.
